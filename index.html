Our Divisions

Four Realms.

Each division is strategically positioned to dominate its market — autonomous in operation, unified in purpose.

Division 01

Aethel Energy

Power · Wheeling PPA · Renewables

Originating and managing Power Purchase Agreements through the wheeling model, our energy arm serves as the long-arc annuity at the heart of the Aethel Energy portfolio. We structure private green energy allocations ranging from 500 kW to 100 MW+ for commercial and industrial clients across South Africa. By securing these long-term agreements, we provide a vital fiscal hedge against rising utility tariffs, delivering clean energy access with zero infrastructure overhead for the end-user.

Division 02

Aethel Connect

Connectivity · Wi-Fi · Enterprise

Delivering high-speed, premium internet connectivity through robust fixed wireless solutions. Aethel Connect operates as a high-growth digital service pillar within the Aethel portfolio—provisioning uncapped digital access directly to underserved communities, high-density residential estates, and commercial enterprises. As a dedicated internet service provider, we streamline the digital experience for the end-user, ensuring seamless connectivity, reliable uptime, and uninterrupted business continuity across South Africa.
